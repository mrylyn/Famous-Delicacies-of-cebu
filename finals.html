Top 3 Special Delicacies in Cebu

LETCHON DE CEBU

“Lechon de Cebu” is a popular Filipino delicacy that is a must-try when visiting Cebu.
It is a whole roasted pig that is stuffed with spices such as tanglad (lemongrass), garlic, onions, and black pepper, which gives it a unique flavor.
The dish is known for its crispy skin and tender meat, and is a staple at many Filipino celebrations and events2.

CHORIZO DE CEBU

Chorizo de Cebu, also known as longganisa de Cebu, is a Filipino pork sausage originating from Cebu.
It is a type of hamonada (sweet) longganisa.
They are distinctively red in color due to the use of achuete seeds. Each link is also usually spherical in shape.

CHICHARON IN CARCAR,CEBU CITY

Chicharon is a popular Filipino snack made from deep-fried pork belly or pork rinds.
It is a well-loved delicacy in Cebu, a province in the Philippines known for its rich culinary heritage .
Chicharon Cebu is considered the best of the best this crunchy delight is one of Cebu's best and tastiest products.
